Meat Lasagna

Description

Low carb lasagna made with hearty meat sauce and pasta. Use your favorite sauce and cheeses to make a culinary delight!

This robust recipe can be assembled and completed in less than an hour, with most of that time being hands off while it bakes. Great for a family dinner!!!

Ingredients

  • Cooked lasagna pasta noodles
  • Hearty Meat Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions
  2. Layer slices in pan
  3. Add a layer of sauce
  4. Add a layer of cheese
  5. Repeat layers 2 more times
  6. Bake for 45 minutes
