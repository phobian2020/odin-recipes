Spicy Eggplant Lasagna

Description

Low carb lasagna made with Eggplant slices instead of pasta. Use your favorite sauce, add some hot peppers or pepper flakes and cheeses to make a culinary delight!

This simple recipe can be assembled and completed in less than an hour, with most of that time being hands off while it bakes. Great for a family dinner!!!

Ingredients

  • Eggplant slices
  • Spicy Sauce(add some hot peppers or red pepper flakes to give it as much kick as you like!)
  • Cheese
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Eggplant slices
  2. Layer slices in pan
  3. Add a layer of sauce
  4. Add a layer of cheese
  5. Repeat layers 2 more times
  6. Bake for 45 minutes
